I joined the Center for Open Data Enterprise primarily to help with the UN SDG National Reporting Initiative project, aimed at helping national organizations share information on implementing reporting platforms for the UN Sustainable Development Goals. After the UN SDG National Reporting Initiative website, I also assisted with some additional project work.

  • Helped drive the design process for the SDG National Reporting Initiative website in cooperation with Threespot, including creating multiple evolving prototypes to guide architectural decisions.
  • Implemented and deployed the final SDG National Reporting Initiative as a Jamstack website powered by Jekyll, Vue, Netlify, ForestAdmin, PostgreSQL, and Sequelize-backed Express APIs. This solution allowed us to leverage a great amount of performance while minimizing costs across multiple dimensions, given the relatively small financial and technical capacity available to the project.
  • Overhauled CODE's Open Data Impact Map website, including schema redesign, bugfixing, code refactoring (eliminated ~20k lines of code), and updating project infrastructure.
  • Implemented a rebranded redesign of CODE's organization website.
  • After project work concluded, I volunteered ongoing time to help maintain the organization website.
